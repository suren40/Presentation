1.Introduction

Winds are caused by the uneven heating of the atmosphere by sun , the irregularities of the earth’s surface, and rotation of the earth.


The terms “wind energy” or “wind power” refers to the use of air flowing throw wind turbines to mechanically power generators for electricity.

HISTORY AND BACKGROUND


  • Wind energy propelled boats along nile river.(5000 B.C)
  • Windmills in china for pumping water.(200 B.C)
  • Windmills were introduced to Europe by the crusaders.( 1300 A.D)
  • The 1st known wind turbine was made in Scotland.( 1887 A.D)
  • A NASA wind turbine program started.(1975)
  • Global wind power capacity reaches 17,400 megawatts.(2000:)
